Session Creation

Method:

createSession()

Input:

userId
applicationId
fingerprint
deviceName
platform
browser
ipAddress
userAgent
expiresAt
accessTokenId

Flow:

1. tìm device theo fingerprint

2. nếu chưa có
      tạo device mới

3. tạo session

4. session active=true

5. lưu lastSeenAt
